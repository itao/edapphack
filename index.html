About

The #EdAppHack is a 2-day experiential and inquiry-based learning opportunity that unites Students, Teachers and App Developers through the theme of STEM. Based on the Hackathon Model, the goal of the weekend event is to empower Student Voice in the creation of apps that support learning needs identified by Student participants. Teachers and App Developers will support each other and collaborate in teams with Students as they imagine, prototype and pitch their app ideas!

Saturday, October 25, 2014 &
Sunday, October 26, 2014

9:00AM - 5:00PM

MaRS Discovery District
Auditorium

101 College Street, Toronto, Ontario M5G 1L7

Facilitators

The #EdAppHack is driven by the vision of 2 Toronto District School Board (TDSB) Teacher-Leaders, Joseph Romano, OCT and Brandon Zoras, OCT. Together as Facilitators, Joseph and Brandon have designed the #EdAppHack to centre around the learning needs of Students, supported by technology, just as they do in their classrooms.
